Column Design Procedure (IS 456:2000)

Given Data

  • Concrete Grade: M25
  • Steel Grade: Fe415
  • Axial Load (Pu): 1500 kN
  • Moments: Mux = 50 kNm, Muy = 30 kNm
  • Column Size: 300 mm x 500 mm
  • Clear Cover: 40 mm

Step 1: Slenderness Check

Effective Length (Both ends restrained): Leff = 0.65 × 3.3m = 2145 mm

Slenderness Ratio = 2145 / 300 = 7.15 < 12 (Short Column)

Step 2: Minimum Eccentricity

emin = (l/500) + (D/30) = 23.27 mm

Minimum Moment = Pu × emin = 34.9 kNm

Step 3: Axial Load Capacity

Gross Area = 300 × 500 = 150000 mm²

Steel Required = 0.8% to 6% of Ag = 1200 mm² to 9000 mm²

Provide 1% Steel = 1500 mm²

Axial Capacity = 1902 kN > 1500 kN (Safe)

Step 4: Combined Load & Moment Check

Use SP 16 Charts (Safe with 1% Reinforcement)

Step 5: Reinforcement Details

  • Longitudinal Reinforcement: 6 Nos. 20 mm Dia Bars (Ast = 1884 mm²)
  • Lateral Ties: 8 mm dia @ 250 mm c/c
